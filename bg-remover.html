Privacy Policy for BG Remover - Background Cut

Effective date: 2026-04-16

Overview

BG Remover - Background Cut ("the app") is A photo background removal and replacement tool for Android. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

Information We Collect

The app itself does not require you to create an account or provide personal information. However, the app integrates third-party services that may collect certain data automatically:

Service Data Collected Purpose
Google AdMob Advertising ID, ad interaction data (impressions, clicks), device information (OS version, screen size, locale) Serving advertisements
Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) Consent preferences GDPR and consent management
Firebase Analytics App usage events, session data, screen views, device information (device model, OS version, app version) App analytics and usage insights
Firebase Crashlytics Crash logs, stack traces, device information (device model, OS version, manufacturer) Crash reporting and stability monitoring
Google Play Billing Purchase history, purchase state, transaction dates Processing in-app purchases
ML Kit Subject Segmentation None — all processing is on-device On-device background removal

On-Device Processing

All photo processing (background removal, editing, filters) happens entirely on your device

Photos are never uploaded to any server

ML models run locally on your device via Google Play Services

Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • Camera: To capture photos for background removal
  • Photo Library Access: To select photos from your gallery for processing

These permissions are used exclusively for the described purposes. No data is uploaded or shared through these permissions.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — serving advertisements
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) — gdpr and consent management
  • Firebase Analytics — app analytics and usage insights
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting and stability monitoring
  • Google Play Billing — processing in-app purchases
  • ML Kit Subject Segmentation — on-device background removal

Data Security

All data transmitted by third-party SDKs is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols (TLS/SSL). The app does not maintain its own servers or databases.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

Under GDPR (European Economic Area):

  • Right to access the data collected about you
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to data processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

Under CCPA (California, USA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to request deletion of personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information — we do not sell your personal information

You can manage ad personalization through your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads). You can also reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads from there.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. Since third-party SDKs may collect device identifiers, the app is intended for users aged 18 and older.
