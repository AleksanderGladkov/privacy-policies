Privacy Policy for SwipePics

Effective date: March 26, 2026

Overview

SwipePics ("the app") is a free photo and video cleanup tool for Android. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

SwipePics does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The app operates entirely on your device. There are no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no ads.

Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • Read Media Images / Read Media Video (Android 13+): Used solely to display your photos and videos within the app so you can decide which to keep or delete.
  • Read External Storage (Android 10–12): Legacy equivalent of the above permission for older Android versions.

These permissions are used exclusively to read and display media on your device. No media is uploaded, copied, or sent anywhere.

Data Storage

The app does not store any data beyond what Android provides by default. No user accounts, no local databases, and no files are created outside your existing media library.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no data is collected from any user, this applies to all age groups equally.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the app's GitHub repository or contact the developer via the Google Play Store listing.
