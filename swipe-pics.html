Privacy Policy for SwipePics

Effective date: 2026-03-26

Overview

SwipePics ("the app") is A photo and video cleanup tool for Android. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

SwipePics does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The app operates entirely on your device. There are no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no ads.

On-Device Processing

All operations happen entirely on your device

No data is collected, stored, or transmitted

Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • Read Media Images / Read Media Video: To display your photos and videos within the app so you can decide which to keep or delete
  • Read External Storage: Legacy equivalent of media access for older Android versions (Android 10–12)

These permissions are used exclusively for the described purposes. No data is uploaded or shared through these permissions.

Data Storage

The app does not store any data beyond what Android provides by default. No user accounts, no local databases, and no files are created outside your existing media library.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. Since no data is collected from any user, this applies to all age groups equally.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer at algladkov.dev@gmail.com or via the Google Play Store listing.
